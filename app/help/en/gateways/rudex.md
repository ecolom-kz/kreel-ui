# Rudex Gateway Service

Rudex is a gateway service built on the Graphene Exchange. A gateway service is responsible for moving cryptocurrencies to and from the Graphene Exchange. They support a wide range of popular assets. You can easily identify those supported by RuDEX because they are prefixed with the word KREEL.*. For example KREEL.BTC, KREEL.EOS, KREEL.USDT, KREEL.GOLOS etc.
